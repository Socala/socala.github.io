Welcome to Bama Wesley

We meet on Tuesday nights at 8 pm at Wesley (505 9th Street, Across from Burke) for worship. This time includes contemporary worship, a short teaching, and is a central point to connect to other activities and happenings throughout the rest of the week.

Our Mission

As a ministry of the United Methodist Church on the campus of the University of Alabama, The Wesley Foundation is committed to making disciples of Jesus Christ for the transformation of the world. Our vision is to be a community where we RISE closer to God through worship, IGNITE a fire inside every student to seek more from God and each other, and to SOAR beyond the walls of our building and out into the world.

What We Do

Rise

Our weekly worship service, held on Tuesdays at 8pm, is called The Rising. At Wesley, we believe we can rise above our past mistakes, worries or insecurities and grow closer to God by seeking Him through worship.

Ignite

"Come to share your story. Stay to hear His." We believe there are two roles we are constantly called to fill: love God and love people. We do this through discipleship and fellowship.

Wade Langer

Campus Pastor

Wade is the founding pastor of The Capstone United Methodist Church. He grew up in Trussville, Alabama and felt a call to ministry in his teenage years. He earned a B.A. degree in Religion from Birmingham-Southern College in 2006, and a Master of Divinity Degree from Candler School of Theology at Emory University in 2009. Before being appointed to start The Capstone UMC, Wade was a youth pastor for five years, and an Associate Minister at First United Methodist Church of Trussville for four years, where he served as the primary pastor for contemporary worship and student ministries.
